Why a Token at All?

            
IZZA Coins power gameplay.
IZZA Token powers the ecosystem.

            
    
              
  • Governance: Vote on new mini-games, IZZA City expansions, creator marketplace rules.
    • 
              
  • Staking: Stake tokens to earn boosted Crafting Credits and seasonal IZZA Coin boosts.
    • 
              
  • Access: Token-gated drops, rare blueprints, early creator tools.
    • 
              
  • Biz Utility: Merchant fee discounts and premium IZZA Pay tiers for holders.
    • 
              
  • Dev Listing: Arcade listings prioritized via stake and quality score.
    •

Launch Roadmap

          
    
            
  • Phase 1 — Testnet Live: Issuer and distributor diagnostics, faucet and trustlines, IZZA City and Arcade earn loop validated.
    • 
            
  • Phase 2 — Staking Beta: Token staking → soft boosts to Crafting Credit earnings, seasonal badges, holder quests.
    • 
            
  • Phase 3 — Governance v1: On-chain votes for new Arcade titles, blueprint rarity schedules, and marketplace fees.
    • 
            
  • Phase 4 — Merchant Utility: IZZA Pay fee rebates for holders, business dashboards with token-gated promos.
    • 
            
  • Phase 5 — Mainnet Mint (33.33B): Final supply and vesting on chain, liquidity bootstrapping, cross-ecosystem creator drops.
    • 
          

        


        

          
Value Loop

          
Players earn Coins → buy Crafting Credits via Pi → craft and trade → creators and merchants thrive. IZZA Token captures upside by governing, staking, and unlocking the best parts of the ecosystem.
