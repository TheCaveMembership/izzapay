Connect with Pi, fund your IZZA BOT testnet account with test Pi, then let the bot trade Pi Testnet tokens based on your time horizon and risk tolerance.

1. Deposit test Pi for trading

When you deposit, a Pi payment is created from your Pi Testnet wallet directly to the shared IZZA BOT trading account. We read the payment details from Pi (PaymentDTO) to credit your IZZA BOT balance and link your wallet.

2. Choose your base bot profile

This is your original IZZA BOT profile. After this, on your IZZA BOT Profile page you will be able to create additional buckets with different settings and allocate your deposit across them.

We aim to return at least 85% of your starting test Pi by the end of your chosen horizon. As markets move, IZZA BOT automatically avoids tokens that no longer fit your selected risk band.
