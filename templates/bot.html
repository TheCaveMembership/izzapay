IZZA BOT Pi Testnet Only

Automated Token Trading

Authenticate with Pi, link your IZZA TESTNET wallet, deposit test Pi to that wallet, then let IZZA BOT trade Pi Testnet tokens based on your time horizon and risk tolerance.

1. Create or link your IZZA wallet

IZZA BOT uses the Pi Testnet (native) balance in your IZZA wallet as trading capital. Create your IZZA wallet, then send TEST PI to it from your Pi Testnet wallet.

Your linked IZZA TESTNET wallet:

On the IZZA wallet page you can create / view your IZZA TESTNET wallet and deposit TEST PI. IZZA BOT automatically reads that balance and shows it on your BOT profile.

2. Choose your base bot profile

This is your original IZZA BOT profile. After this, on your IZZA BOT Profile page you will be able to create additional buckets with different settings and allocate your test Pi deposits across them.

We aim to return at least 85% of your starting test Pi by the end of your chosen horizon. As markets move, IZZA BOT automatically avoids tokens that no longer fit your selected risk band.

After this step you can manage buckets and allocations on your IZZA BOT Profile.
