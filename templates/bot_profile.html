This is your IZZA BOT profile. Your IZZA TESTNET wallet holds your test Pi. When you deposit into a bucket, test Pi moves from your IZZA wallet into the bot wallet and increases that bucket’s balance. When you withdraw from a bucket, it moves back to your IZZA wallet.

To unlock your very first deposit into the Default Bot Bucket, the bot charges a one-time fee of 5 IZZA from your IZZA wallet to the bot hot wallet. Every additional bucket you create also costs 5 IZZA.

IZZA BOT is currently in test mode on Pi Testnet. In crypto, prices can move very quickly and tokens can crash. There is no guarantee that any deposit is safe, and the bot cannot promise to stop losses at a specific level. Only deposit test Pi that you are fully prepared to lose.

Each bucket has a downside target based on its risk level. The bot logic is designed to do its best to keep drawdowns around these ranges for that bucket, but extreme market moves can still push losses beyond them.

Approximate target max loss per risk:
• Low risk: target up to 15% drawdown
• Medium risk: target up to 30% drawdown
• High risk: target up to 45% drawdown

If you are not willing to risk up to this loss or more on this bucket, do not deposit funds into it yet.

Creating a new bucket costs 5 IZZA from your IZZA wallet to the bot hot wallet. Make sure you hold at least 5 IZZA before creating another bucket.
